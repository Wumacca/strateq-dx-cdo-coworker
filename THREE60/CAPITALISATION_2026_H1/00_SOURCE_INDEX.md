## 3. Evidence Control Notes

The Board deck must use this source index as its control reference.

No financial, adoption, regulatory, or maturity movement claim should be presented as fully achieved unless the supporting evidence is linked or available in the closeout pack.

Where evidence is incomplete, the wording must use one of the following controlled positions:

- `subject to final finance reconciliation`
- `supported by current programme reporting`
- `pending final adoption gate evidence`
- `enabled but not yet measured`
- `controlled in-flight initiative`
