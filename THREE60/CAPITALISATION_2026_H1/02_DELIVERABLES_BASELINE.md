# 02_DELIVERABLES_BASELINE.md

# THREE60 Operations Digital Capitalisation Programme  
## Deliverables Baseline

**Programme period:** January to July 2026  
**Purpose:** To establish the approved and emergent deliverable baseline for programme closeout comparison.

---

## 1. Baseline Control Position

The deliverables baseline is split into two categories:

1. **Approved capitalisation roadmap deliverables** — initiatives included in the original capitalisation programme.
2. **Emergent controlled deliverables** — additional priority work absorbed during the programme without additional capital request.

This distinction is important for Board reporting. The closeout report must show that the programme delivered the approved roadmap while also absorbing additional operationally important work through controlled governance.

## 4. Deliverable Completion Categories

| Category | Definition |
|---|---|
| Complete / Live | Delivered and in use. |
| Complete / Adoption Gate Approved | Delivered and passed post-adoption review. |
| Live / Rollout Continuing | Implemented in part; rollout continues as controlled BAU or functional schedule. |
| In Progress | Delivery has started and remains governed. |
| Controlled Delay | Not completed to original schedule due to higher-priority operational constraints; not uncontrolled failure. |
| Pipeline / Next Phase | Scoped or selected for next six-month capitalisation request. |
