# THREE60 Operations Digital Capitalisation Programme  
## Actual Delivery and Variance Register

**Programme period:** January to July 2026  
**Purpose:** To compare actual programme delivery against the approved capitalisation baseline and controlled emergent scope.

---

## 1. Executive Delivery Position

The programme has delivered the first six-month Operations digital maturity uplift within the approved capitalisation envelope.

The June 2026 closeout position confirms:

- initiatives are under budget at programme level, subject to final finance record;
- emergent work has been absorbed without additional capital request;
- Reg 5 KPI reporting was delivered with no observations raised;
- COSHH 365 and Omega Incident Management passed adoption gates;
- the majority of approved initiatives are complete or in final rollout;
- Salus Suite remains in controlled delay due to business-critical Safety Case priorities;
- Action Management Quality rollout is controlled by functional availability and priority;
- CMMS and Training & Competence are positioned for next-phase implementation;
- the governance, delivery, and engagement model is now established.
