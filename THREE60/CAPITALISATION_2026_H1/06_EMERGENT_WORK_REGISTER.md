## 3. Emergent Work Control Rules

Emergent work must meet the following control tests before being reported as absorbed programme value:

| Control Test | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Business reason | The need must be linked to risk, assurance, transition, cost, efficiency, or operational control. |
| Ownership | Functional owner and digital owner must be identified. |
| Route | The work must enter the hopper, delivery tracker, or approved initiative route. |
| Cost treatment | Cost must be absorbed, separately approved, or deferred into next phase. |
| Delivery impact | Impact on approved roadmap schedule must be recorded. |
| Evidence | Output, decision, adoption, or progress evidence must be retained. |

The approved programme also absorbed a controlled set of emergent priorities, including BP Andrew / NEO transition support, Reg 5 support, COSHH 365, and additional reporting and workflow requirements.
