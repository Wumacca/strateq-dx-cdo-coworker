## 1. Executive Ask

**Decision required:** TBC

The next capitalisation request should be framed as the continuation and scaling of the enterprise digital management system established through the January to July 2026 programme.

This is not a request to fund a disconnected list of tools.

It is a request to scale the controlled operating model now proven through:

- governed initiative intake;
- hopper prioritisation;
- delivery control;
- adoption gates;
- benefits evidence;
- KPI and assurance reporting;
- action management;
- Duty Holder support;
- workflow digitisation;
- transition support;
- maturity movement tracking.

---

## 2. Strategic Rationale

The first six-month programme established the foundation.

The next six-month programme should scale, harden, and extend that foundation.

The Board rationale should be:

| Rationale | Board Message |
|---|---|
| Enterprise Risk & Assurance | The business now requires continued digital control to support Duty Holder reporting, action governance, system ownership, and regulatory readiness. |
| Duty Holder / Regulatory Expectation | Reporting, COSHH, action management, incident investigation, and KPI governance are now part of the assurance environment. |
| Operating Model & Governance | The intake-to-hopper-to-delivery-to-adoption model must be institutionalised. |
| Scalability & Sustainability | The business must avoid reverting to isolated tools, spreadsheets, and local workarounds. |
| Efficiency / Automation / Tooling | The next phase should reduce manual effort and strengthen data, workflow, reporting, and system integration. |
